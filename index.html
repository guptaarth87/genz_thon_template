GenZ-Thon is a 7-day long competition based on problem-solving over 5 domains of Computer Science. The domain names are as follows -

  • Web Development
  • Game Development
  • Internet of Things
  • Machine Learning
  • Mobile App Development

Roadmap

Teams would register for the domains of their interest. Team size could vary from 2-4 members per team with a registration fee of INR 2001 per team.

Upon registration, the primary contact would be mailed regarding the problem statements corresponding to the selected domain. As soon asthe mails are sent, competition would start, i.e. the countdown for 7 days would start from there. Participants are required to inform about the problem Statement they are working upon within 3 days after starting of competition

All the participants will work on the solution for their problemsfor 7 days continuously. Technical experts for each domain would be provided so that teams can contact them upon issues.

Teams would be required to work on their project through the GitHub account of MUACM. Upon completion of the project, the solutions would be required to be submitted. The submission can occur in two ways -

  1. Create a user of MUACM on Github and mandate every team to add that one as a collaborator to the project. Upon completion of the project, the repo would be automatically cloned and submission would be evaluated.
  2. Teams would be given the liberty to work on their solutions in a purely private repo. Upon completion of the project, they would be required to submit a zip file of the code and supporting materials on a Google drive. The submissions would then be evaluated accordingly.
We will be closing submissions exactly after 168 hours (24*7)

After the final day deadline, the panel of judges will start filtering out the best solutions from each domain. The shortlisted teams would be called for a presentation session with the panel.

The judges based on the presentation will decide the best solution from each domain. Only a single solution from each domain is eligible for cash prizes. The best solutions which are not rewarded will also be eligible for perks such as swags, stickers, internships, project opportunities, etc.

  • Expert talks, activities will be carried out throughout the competition and will be having corresponding rewards
  • Gaming activities and crowd interaction with team MUACM would also be carried on.

Rewards Structure

Cash Rewards

Best Project in each domain can be rewarded upto INR 10,000 cash prize. Rewards can vary on the basis of sponsorship received.
Coupons/Swags/Vouchers/Gift Cards -:

Will/Can be provided to all the teams shortlisted for presentation.
Internships/Project Opportunities-:

Can be offered by the sponsors to all the participating teams on the basis of their performance and project. We will be sharing all the relevant and fully functional projects with the sponsor for evaluation.
